Home: Services

Find dignity, safety and hope

Across the state and beyond, Lutheran Social Service of Minnesota connects people with opportunities to live and work with dignity, safety and hope. Services span physical health and safety and emotional wellbeing.

Camps, Retreats & Event Centers

Our event spaces, team-building opportunities and camps help build community and support children with unique needs.

Disaster Recovery

We partner with Minnesota communities in long-term disaster recovery. Camp Noah is offered nationally in disaster-impacted communities to help children cope and heal.

Families

From parenting support to foster care to marriage preparation, services strengthen families and promote resiliency.

Financial & Employment Services

A full range of services, including debt management and career development, promote long-term financial stability for people in all stages of life.

Housing & Assistance

Families and individuals on the path to stable, affordable housing are served through financial assistance, safe housing and case management.

Mental Health

Compassionate, personalized therapy services and programs support children and adults on the journey toward healthier, happier lives.

Military & Veterans

Community, social and healthcare services for veterans, service members and their families are designed with respect for and understanding of military culture.

Older Adults

Services encourage independence, well-being and relationships and give older adults the opportunity to contribute to community.

People with Disabilities

From care coordination to employment to in-home support, services empower people with disabilities to shape and live the lives they imagine for themselves.

Refugees

As individuals and families fleeing from persecution start anew in Minnesota, we walk beside them in support with resettlement, immigration and employment services.

Youth & Young Adults

Services keep youth safe and support a healthy transition to adulthood through classes, foster care, school programs and mentoring.

Youth Homelessness

From shelters and meals to housing and preventive services, LSS is committed to ending youth homelessness in the regions we serve.

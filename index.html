Because practicing compassion will change the world

If we serve where we are, with what we have, it will make a difference. This belief has grown Lutheran Social Service of Minnesota into one of the largest and the oldest human service nonprofits in the state. Each year, we have the opportunity to connect one in 65 Minnesotans to community, health, safety, housing and prosperity through personalized support.

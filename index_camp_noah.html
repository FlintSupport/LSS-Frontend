About Camp Noah

Process, play and heal.

Children are encouraged to face their fears, grieve their losses and plan for an amazing future. Elementary-age children in communities impacted by disaster and trauma attend for free.

Get Involved

Space for kids to be kids.

Kids across the country are experiencing the power of hope and healing in communities that need it most. Camp Noah is a nationally acclaimed program that helps children process their disaster or trauma experience through creative activities and play.
